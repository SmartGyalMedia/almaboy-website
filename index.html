The artist

Kirton "Alma Boy" Roberts

Unfiltered energy.
International reach.

Hailing from Grenada, Alma Boy is a singer, songwriter, and producer whose energy captivates audiences wherever he plays. His music brings unfiltered energy built for the road and the stage.

His catalog moves between highs and hard truths, party anthems, love songs, and real talk, all delivered with the same unmistakable charisma. Equal parts fete host and headline act, he's built for festival mainstages and international rooms alike.

He doesn't perform for a crowd. He performs with one.
